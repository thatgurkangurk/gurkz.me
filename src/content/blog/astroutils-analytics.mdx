Ever wanted an easy way to set up analytics for your Astro app?

Well, look no further!

@astroutils/analytics makes it easy to setup analytics in your app,
and will be compatible with many analytics providers *(currently, only Umami)*

Want to read more? Go to the [docs](https://docs.gurkz.me/packages/analytics)

Enjoy!
